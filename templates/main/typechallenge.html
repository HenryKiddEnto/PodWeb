Game Objective

Sort all Pokémon into teams of three!

Pokémon that share a weakness cannot be on the same team.

Basic Controls

  • Drag & Drop: Move Pokémon between teams
  • Right-Click: View type weaknesses and alternate formes

Teams

  • Teams turn green when complete
  • The remainder box holds 1-2 Pokémon when the total isn't divisible by 3
  • Click Check to verify your solution
  • Click Reset to start over

Regions

  • Each region includes the full regional dex.
  • Regions marked by ★ contain DLC Pokémon (e.g. Isle of Armor, ZA Hyperspace)
